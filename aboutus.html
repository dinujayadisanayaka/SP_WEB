Who we are?



Sp Home Construction (PVT) Ltd has been registered as limited liability company to carry on the business in 18 July 2018 & got the registration at "samagam medura"as a civil and mechanical construction.

We start in our furniture manufacturing process since 2017 and our main part of business is personal house and commercial building construction so we have good potential to develop our modern funiture concept trough civil construction works.

We aim at being one of the best furniture manufactures of all kind of furniture designed with functionality, high quality and affordable prices in mind and save trees to the future. We mainly target urban areas to promote out polymer base eco sheet to fulfill their all kind of furniture requirements in high quality. And also We plan to manufacture furniture using MDF and Melamine sheets to satisfy middle class society's furnituirements.
